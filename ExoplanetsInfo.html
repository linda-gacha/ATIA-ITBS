01. Kepler-16b

Kepler-16b orbits two stars, making it a circumbinary planet — something like a real-world "Tatooine" from Star Wars.

ORBIT PERIOD: 229 Days
Distance from Earth: ~200 light-years
Mass: 0.33 Jupiter masses
Temperature: -73°C to -101°C
Atmosphere: Hydrogen, Helium

02. Proxima Centauri b

Proxima Centauri b is the closest known exoplanet to Earth and orbits within its star’s habitable zone.

ORBIT PERIOD: 11.2 days
Distance from Earth: 4.24 light-years
Mass: 1.17 Earth masses
Temperature: -39°C
Atmosphere: Unknown
